portfolio

GRUBMEET

Don't want to go all the way downtown to meet your friend for dinner? Grubmeet allows you to find the best dining options that are at the midway point between you and your friends. Enter in as many addresses as you want, and Grubmeet will recommend options that are convenient for everyone.

GLOBETROTTER

Globetrotter is a web-based travel app that allows you to log your travels and see where you rank among the top travelers of the world. Globetrotter will calculate the distances of your trips as well as your total distance traveled. User profiles also include maps that show all the locations you have traveled to and from.
