GRUBMEET

Don't want to go all the way downtown to meet your friend for dinner? Grubmeet allows you to find the best dining options that are at the midway point between you and your friends. Enter in as many addresses as you want, and Grubmeet will recommend options that are convenient for everyone.

GA SEARCH ENGINE

If you've ever visited General Assembly, it's hard not to notice their massive job boards that sit in the lobbies of their workspaces. As someone who checked the board regularly, I decided to create a digital version. The job cards you see on the site are nearly identical to those you can find at GA.

The online GA Search Engine limits registration to only those affiliated with GA, thus preserving the tight-knit community aspect of the offline version. THIS IS NOT AN ACTUAL GENERAL ASSEMBLY PRODUCT, just a fun project I worked on in GA's WDI class. If you'd like to see what's behind the curtain, use code '1234' when registering.

FIRST CLASS AIRLINES

This application handles both client-side and admin-side functionality for fake airline FirstClass. Three teammates and I worked to build out the web app which simulates some of the basic functions that an actual airline would desire on their web sites. Our app allows admins to manage a plane inventory and flight schedule. Non-admin users are able to book flights and reserve seats using a nifty hand-made seat picking chart.

GLOBETROTTER

Globetrotter is a web-based travel app that allows you to log your travels and see where you rank among the top travelers of the world. Globetrotter will calculate the distances of your trips as well as your total distance traveled. User profiles also include maps that show all the locations a user has traveled to and from.
