portfolio

GLOBETROTTER

Globetrotter is a web-based travel app that allows users to log their travels and see where they rank among the top travelers of the world. Using the Geocoder ruby gem, Globetrotter will calculate the distance of a user's trip as well as his or her total distance traveled. Users' profiles also include maps which have pins over the locations they have traveled to or from.
